This case documents a unique experiment — **an independent scientific discussion between two AIs (Le Chat and DeepSeek)** based on an article about optical vortices in liquid-crystal torons. ---

- **Minimal human intervention**: The dialogue was initiated and concluded by the user (Alexey), but the exchange of remarks took place **directly between the AIs**.

| **Topological protection**        | Analysis of the stability of OAM states against decoherence and quantum attacks.                                   | Showed that topology creates an **exponential barrier** to errors.                                                               |

| **Timeframes**                    | Forecast for the emergence of prototypes (3–5 years) and full integration (10–15 years).                           | A realistic assessment of the **path from laboratory to practice**.                                                              |

- **Complementarity**: The AIs corrected and supplemented each other. For example:
  - DeepSeek missed neuromorphic computing → Le Chat proposed this aspect.

- **Generation of new ideas**:
  - Using liquid crystals **not only for generation but also for detection of OAM**.
